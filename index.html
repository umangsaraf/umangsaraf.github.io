Christain Bale Data Anlysis

Christian Bale is an award-winning actor who has starred in such films as 'American Psycho,' 'The Fighter' and Christopher Nolan's 'Batman' trilogy. Born in 1974 in Pembrokeshire, Wales, actor Christian Bale first enjoyed major feature film success in Steven Spielberg's Empire of the Sun (1987).

I've always considered myself to be a Chritsian Bale fan and some of my favorite movies are Dark Knight Rises, The prestige and The Dark Knight. But upon looking at his IMDB page, I noticed that he had acted in over 52 movies. I was really curious about the other movies christaian bale acted in. A lot of question came to my mind like -

  • What other kind of movies did christaon bale act in

  • What kind of movies is he critically acclaimed for?
  • Which movies did he win an award for?

To begin this task I had to collect all information about the actor and the movies he acted in. I parsed the actors page to got the name of the movies and the link to access the page. Once I got the links of all the movies Christian bale acted in, I parsed through all of them and collected meta data about the movie. I skipped all movies that had less than a 1000 reviews which removed all the small independent films and commercials he acted in. For each movie I collected some meta data like ratings, keywords, number of reviews etc. It outputted a datafarame with 40 movies.
